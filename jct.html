Welcome Home to Jubilee

Every great story begins with a simple dream. Ours started with the belief that coffee and tea aren't just beverages - they're moments of connection, comfort, and joy.

After nearly three decades, we're proud to continue the legacy my parents began with love, dedication, and a homemade coffee cart that brought joy to communities across Alabama. Now, my mother and I are working together to bring Jubilee back to life for a new generation.

Explore Our Coffee Family

A Legacy Reborn

The Original Dream (1995)

Nearly 30 years ago, my parents started Jubilee Coffee & Tea with nothing but passion, a small mail-order business, and a homemade coffee cart. They brought the joy of exceptional coffee to local festivals and parades across Alabama, building relationships one cup at a time.

The Foundation They Built

With every festival, every parade, every carefully packaged mail order, my parents weren't just selling coffee - they were creating connections. They understood that great coffee brings people together, creates memories, and becomes part of family traditions.

Rising Again (2025)

Today, my mother and I are honored to breathe new life into the dream she and my father created. While the world has changed, the heart of Jubilee remains the same - exceptional coffee and tea, genuine care for our customers, and the belief that every cup should be a moment of pure joy.

"From a homemade cart to your home - the Jubilee spirit lives on."

- Pat & Will Scanland, Continuing the Family Legacy
